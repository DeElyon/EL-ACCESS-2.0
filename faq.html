Frequently Asked Questions

Account Management

How do I reset my password?
Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.
How can I update my profile information?
Log in to your account, click on your profile icon, and select "Edit Profile" to update your information.

Courses

How do I access my enrolled courses?
After logging in, click on "My Courses" in the navigation menu to see all your enrolled courses.
Can I download course materials for offline viewing?
Yes, most course materials can be downloaded for offline viewing. Look for the download icon next to the content.

Payments

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for course payments.
How do I request a refund?
Contact our support team within 30 days of purchase to request a refund. Terms and conditions apply.
